Main concept

  • City tours using local transportation
  • User can choose from local attractions or select recommended tours
  • Automatic routing between locations
  • Scheduling the whole day for the user
  • Estimating travel times and duration of the visits
  • Filter locations by categories
  • Easy purchase for the transportation ticket
  • Travellers, tourists, local citizens

Business model

  • Free app
  • Extended features with a paid app?
  • Make deals with companies to promote events
  • Make deals with local transportation companies

Scalability

  • Any location where these kinds of APIs are available
  • Shopping
  • Licensing
